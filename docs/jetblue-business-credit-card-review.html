JetBlue Business Credit Card Review

By Triston Martin Updated on 2023-01-10 00:00:00
The JetBlue Business Credit Card rewards businesses with free checked bags, discounted JetBlue Mint fares, and JetBlue TrueBlue points on all purchases. It also offers travel and purchase protections and a 12-billing cycle with 0% intro APR on purchases. The card's first-year $99 annual fee is waived. Mastercard-issued Barclaycard issues it.

The JetBlue Business Credit Card is a rewards credit card designed specifically for businesses. It is issued by Barclaycard and is part of the Mastercard network. The card offers a variety of benefits to its users, including the ability to earn JetBlue TrueBlue points on all purchases, free checked bags on JetBlue flights, and discounted JetBlue Mint fares. After the initial 12 billing cycles, the card's regular purchase APR ranges from 15.74% to 24.74%. (variable). The card has a $99 annual fee, which is waived for the first year. In addition to these benefits, the JetBlue Business Credit Card offers a variety of travel and purchase protections, including travel accident insurance, car rental collision damage waiver, and extended warranty protection.

Jetblue Business Credit Card Earning Rewards

The jetblue credit card business lets you earn TrueBlue points on all purchases. Cardholders earn 3 points per $1 spent on JetBlue, 2 points per $1 spent at restaurants and office supply stores, and 1 point per $1 spent on all other purchases. Flights, hotels, car rentals, and other travel expenses can be redeemed with these points. Cardholders receive 6,000 bonus points on their account anniversary. In addition to earning points on purchases, the JetBlue Business Credit Card offers several other ways to earn rewards. After spending $1,000 in the first 90 days, cardholders receive a 40,000-point bonus. They can also get 10,000 points when they open a JetBlue Business Card and make a purchase within 90 days.

Jetblue Business Credit Card Travel Perks

JetBlue Business Credit Cards offer travel benefits. JetBlue allows cardholders and up to three companions free checked bags. For frequent travelers, this benefit can save businesses hundreds of dollars in baggage fees. JetBlue Business Credit Cards offer discounted JetBlue Mint fares and free checked bags. JetBlue Mint, the airline's premium class, offers lie-flat seats, personalized service, and many in-flight amenities. JetBlue Business Credit Card holders can save 10% on JetBlue Mint fares.

Jetblue Business Credit Card Fees And Apr

The jetblue business credit card login has a $99 annual fee, waived the first year. Businesses that fly JetBlue frequently may find this fee worth it. The first 12 billing cycles have a 0% intro APR on purchases. This can help companies to make large purchases and pay them off over time. Creditworthiness-based APRs range from 15.74% to 24.74% after the intro period. Balance transfers and cash advances are not eligible for the intro APR offer and will accrue interest at the regular APR from the transaction date.

Benefits And Protections:

In addition to its rewards and travel perks, the JetBlue Business Credit Card also offers a variety of benefits and protections to its users. These include:

  • Travel accident insurance: If you or a covered family member suffer a loss while traveling on a common carrier (such as an airplane), you may be eligible for coverage under the travel accident insurance benefit.
  • Car rental collision damage waiver: This benefit covers damages to a rental car if you charge the entire rental cost to your JetBlue Business Credit Card and decline the rental company's collision damage waiver.
  • Extended warranty protection: This benefit extends the manufacturer's warranty on items purchased with your JetBlue Business Credit Card for up to one additional year.
  • Purchase protection: Within the first 90 days after making a purchase with your Westjet Business Credit Card, you could be eligible for the benefit known as purchase safeguard if the item is damaged or stolen.
  • Fraud protection: The JetBlue Business Credit Card offers 24/7 fraud monitoring and security, so you can know that your account is secure.

Customer Service:

Barclaycard, the JetBlue Business Credit Card issuer, is known for its excellent customer service. Cardholders can access customer service 24/7 through the Barclaycard website or by calling the customer service number on the back of their card. In addition, cardholders can manage their account online or through the Barclaycard mobile app, which allows them to view account activity, pay their bill, and redeem rewards.

Conclusion

Overall, the JetBlue Business Credit Card is a good choice for businesses that frequently fly with JetBlue and want to earn rewards on their purchases. The ability to earn JetBlue TrueBlue points on all purchases and receive free checked bags on JetBlue flights and discounted JetBlue Mint fares can make this card valuable for businesses that frequently travel for work. In addition, the card provides a 0% introductory annual percentage rate (APR) on purchases made within the first 12 billing cycles, which can be useful for businesses that must make substantial purchases but pay them off over time. The annual fee of $99 is waived for the first year, which is a nice added benefit. Additionally, the card offers a variety of uses and protections, including travel accident insurance, car rental collision damage waiver, and extended warranty protection.
