瑞士布里恩茨湖终极旅行指南

By Christy Woodrow Updated on March 8, 2023
In this guide, we’ll take you through everything you need to know about visiting Lake Brienz, including how to get there, the best time to visit, things to do, where to stay and eat, and what to pack! Switzerland is known for its stunning natural beauty and one of the most breathtaking places in the country is Lake Brienz. This stunning lake is surrounded by majestic mountains and lush green forests, making it a perfect spot to explore nature. With its crystal clear waters and picturesque views, it’s a great place to relax and unwind. Plus, there are plenty of activities available such as swimming, sailing, fishing or just taking a stroll along the shoreline. Whether you’re looking for adventure or relaxation, Lake Brienz has something to offer!
